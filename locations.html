Heaven is Here

Singapore

Marina Bay Sands – An iconic luxury hotel with a rooftop infinity pool, observation deck, and stunning views of the city skyline.
Gardens by the Bay – A futuristic nature park featuring the famous Supertree Grove, Flower Dome, and Cloud Forest

Seychelles

Praslin Island & Vallée de Mai – Home to the rare Coco de Mer palm and stunning beaches like Anse Lazio, perfect for nature lovers.
La Digue Island – A paradise with crystal-clear waters, charming local culture, and scenic cycling trails.

Hawaii

Waikiki Beach – Famous for its golden sands, clear waters, and vibrant nightlife, this beach is a must-visit for surfers and sun-seekers.
Haleakalā National Park – Home to the world’s largest dormant volcano, offering breathtaking sunrise views above the clouds..

A historic amphitheater that once hosted gladiator battles.

Sydney

An architectural masterpiece and cultural hub.
Best time to visit: September to November or March to May.
